Research

I'm currently writing my PhD in biostatistics at the Medical University of Vienna. Martin Posch is my supervisor. As these things go I still don't have a pronounced topic. To get an idea of my research areas see my list of publications, or alternatively the list of conference presentations. For a detailed account of my professional experience see my current CV.

Software

I (co)-maintain several r-packages:

orQA
a small package for quality assessment of order restricted high dimensional data. For more info see also the supplementary website of our publication.
gMCP
a package for designing, conducting and evaluating graph-based multiple testing procedures gMCP. I maintain the part regarding parametric and flexible adaptive tests, whereas Kornelius Rohmeyer works on the GUI and the sequentially rejective Bonferroni stuff.
